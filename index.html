FARMER'S MENTAL HEALTH

About Wellbeing

Farmers tend to be very aware about the welfare of their stock but tend to neglect their own welfare. There are a lot of stressul poblems that farmers face every day and these problems can all too easily mount up. This, aswell as the stigma of having to tough it out alone, can make it difficult for farmers to deal with this stress. Fortunately there are plenty of friendly faces in the farming community and there are plenty of people willing to help each other out. So lets take time to see how others are doing, offer our support for problems, dont be afraid tom accept help and lets turn striving to thriving.

About Events

It is important to socialise and connect with people around us. It's really good to be able to spend time enjoying ourselves with the company of people we like. Having this connection with other people can also be really helpful if we do start feeling down about things. Then we can have those people around us that will support us. By chatting to those people you will find that you're not the only person dealing with these issues. How you’re feeling is completely normal and your not alone in it. Other people who have already endured these problems can also give you support on how to get through it. They can share with you how they did it and give you advice for you.

  • REACHING OUT

  • No one has to tough it out on ther own. Their are plenty of people out there willing to help out where they can. Sometimes we can get stuck on our problems and it can be hard to find the solution. But no one can find the solution to every problem by themselves and sometimes just a bit of outside perspective from an experienced person can give us a new and refreshing approach.
