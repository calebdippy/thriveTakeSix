  • About Wellbeing

  • Groundswell NZ is a grassroots volunteer-driven advocacy group seeking a halt to, and rewrite of, unworkable regulations which unfairly impact farmers and rural communities.

  • It all started with a tractor protest in Gore, but with overwhelming national support our movement has grown into a stronger advocacy voice for rural communities, seeking solutions to environmental issues which are effective and that can be tailored to regional differences.
