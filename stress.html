STRESS

Problem

Stress and burnout is a mount up of stress that leads to physical and emotional exhaustion. This exhaustion slowly creeps up on farmers and can be hard to notice until it's too late and it becomes a serious issue.

Solution
  • Talk to others - Talking to others can help farmers to see a new perspective and to support them.
  • Reduce stimulation - When you are stressed your body looks for stimulation which can lead to restlessness, lack of sleep and even more stress. To prevent over stimulating try breathing techniques or exercise
  • Think in helpful ways - acknowledge the problems being faced. Then try and think about them from different angles or perspectives. Figure out if other are dealing with similar issues and see what can be controlled
