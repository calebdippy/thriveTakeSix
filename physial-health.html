PHYSICAL HEALTH

Problem

The nature of farming has changed with the use of machinery and technology leading to less physical activity. Adding physical movement into your working day helps to prevent health issues and ensures you have the fitness level for important annual events like calving, which still require physical and cardio endurance. Mental health problems can often be linked back to physical problems so by keeping your physical health in check you are also doing favours for your mental health.

Solution
  • Make it a habit - Find a time in your day that works best for you. It could be first thing in the morning or when you break for lunch. Doing it at the same time as part of your daily routine helps it become a habit.
  • Start slowly - The key is to start slowly and add a new movement each week. You can repeat the routine more times and add additional movements as you get stronger.
  • Involve others - If you work with others, do it as a team before you start your day. Think of it like a team warm-up before a game, it will energise you and help prevent injuries.
